Week 2 – Long Live the Web

In “Long Live the Web” by Tim Berners Lee, Tim explains that the web was built to be free and open for everyone, but that today it’s at risk because big companies and governments want more control. He says the web’s power comes from being decentralized, where anyone can share and connect information without needing permission. Reading this made me realize how much I rely on the web every day for things like learning, working with peers, and finding and curating new ideas. If it becomes closed off or controlled it could stop being a space where people can freely share and connect. Tim’s message is that protecting the web is not just about technology but about protecting freedom and opportunity for people everywhere, and I definitely resonate with and support his message.
